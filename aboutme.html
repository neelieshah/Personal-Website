ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Neelie Shah. Right now I'm a Sophomore at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in Terre Haute, Indiana. Currently, I'm working towards getting a double major in Computer Science and Software Engineering with a minor in Data Science. While I love CS and its related fields, I also love being involved in all sorts of other things! On campus, I am involved in the South Asian Student Alliance, the Student Government Association, and my sorority, Chi Omega. While I'm not on campus, I enjoy traveling, cooking, and baking! CHI OMEGA (ΧΩ)

When I joined college, I never thought I would join a sorority. It was only once I realized what a strong group of role models and female engineers that Chi Omega had, did I decide to rush. Since then, I have come to appreciate the sisterhood that Chi O gives me and understand its importance. At a school that is over 70% male and a major that is ever more predominantly male, knowing I have sisters to rely on through it all is comforting. Currently, I am an Assitant Community Service Director for my chapter.

TRAVELING

Exploring other cultures has always been important to me because of how rejuvinating and educational it is. Thankfully I've been lucky enough to be able to travel to many different countries!

JAPAN


Japan was unlike any place I had ever been to. From the second I landed, I was shocked by how clean and high-tech everything was. The food was one of a kind and the green scenery in all the Japanese Gardens was relaxing. Japan also unique in its fashion and the shopping scene was loud and unapologetically iteself.

PORTUGAL


Portugal was the quintessential European country. Everything from its distinct building to its interesting palaces made unmistakenly European! The food was flavorful, the people were kind, and each city had its own charm. All of this together made Portugal the perfect summer destination!
